Frequently Asked Questions

We answer directly the most common questions about switching to Lumi:

How does the free data migration work?
You import your entire client list from your old system in seconds! Simply export your data as a CSV file (from Vagaro, Mindbody, or Fresha) and upload it directly through the Lumi control panel. Everything is 100% automated, instant, and secure.
Is there a contract or cancellation fee?
Zero lock-in. We believe in the quality of our system. You can subscribe, test, and cancel anytime with 1 click directly from the control panel — no annoying phone calls or bureaucracy.
Does Lumi work well on my phone?
Yes! Lumi was designed Mobile-First. That means it was optimized for fast performance on any smartphone brand. You and your team can add clients, record sales, and check the schedule right from the palm of your hand — with no lag.
Do I need to install any app?
No! Lumi is an ultra-lightweight cloud platform. Just open the dashboard link in your phone or PC browser. You can also save the site as an icon on your home screen just like a regular app — taking up zero storage space on your device.
How do clients use the self-booking feature?
You receive a unique, personalized link for your studio or salon. Paste it in your Instagram bio or set it as an auto-reply on WhatsApp. Clients click it, see available slots, and book their appointment in 10 seconds. The booking appears on your screen instantly!
